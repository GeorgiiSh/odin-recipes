Guacamole

Home

Description

A creamy, tangy dip bursting with fresh lime, onion, and cilantro—perfect for chips or tacos.

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 1 Tbsp lime juice
  • 2 Tbsp finely chopped red onion
  • 1 Tbsp chopped cilantro
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Mash avocados with lime juice, season with salt and pepper.
  2. Fold in onion and cilantro; serve immediately with chips or tacos.

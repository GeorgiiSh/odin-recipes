Pancakes

Home

Description

Fluffy, golden‑brown cakes that soak up butter and syrup for a comforting breakfast or brunch.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup (125 g) all‑purpose flour
  • 1 Tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 cup (240 ml) milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 Tbsp melted butter (plus extra for the pan)

Steps

  1. Whisk dry ingredients in a bowl; in another bowl, beat milk, egg, and butter.
  2. Pour wet into dry; stir until just combined (some lumps are OK).
  3. Heat a buttered skillet over medium; pour ¼ cup batter per pancake.
  4. Cook until bubbles form, flip, cook 1 min more. Serve with syrup or toppings.
